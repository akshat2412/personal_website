About Me

Hi! My name is Akshat. I am a software developer and I love to build software that ease life for people.  Looking for an opportunity, I would love to hear from you.

I am Computer Science B-Tech student from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha university, and graduating this year. I started with learning Data Structures and Algorithms, which opened up a new world for me. I took a little detour after that and delved into the world of development, did various courses on web development and met some amazing people.

I like building web apps and learning new technologies. You can find my projects in the 'Projects' section. I have built single page applications as well as full fledged machine learning based web apps. When given an opportunity, I would love to work on complex problems, that matter in day to day lives of people, and which have the power to bring a change in lifestyle of people and society.

Apart from my professional life, I love to play Cricket, video games, singing and drawing. I also like surfing on the Internet, which also helped me in deciding some of my projects. I believe that a good engineer should have great searching skills on the Internet, and should know where to look for answers.

I have interned with Josh Technology Group in the summer of 2017 where I was among the 4 people selected from our batch. I also did an internship at HT Media Labs as a software developer. People who I worked along with describe me as a quick learner, hard working and curious.

Currently, I am about to finish my graduation in a few months. I am actively looking for an opportunity where I can use my skills, which provides a great learning and dynamic environment, and pushes me to be better. I would love to hear from you about any opportunities or feedback.
